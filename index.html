About 3 Axis Works

Built on precision.
Driven by craft.

3 Axis Works was founded by two brothers who share a lifelong passion for design and an obsession with the finer details. That eye for craft is built into everything we do — from the way we review your files to the finish on the final part.

Based in Perth, we work with engineers, designers, and businesses to bring complex geometries to life with the accuracy and surface finish that production demands. Whether you're iterating on a prototype or running a short production batch, we handle every step in-house.

FDM Printing

Resin / SLA

Materials

The right material
for every application

Not all prints are the same. We carry a curated range of materials to match the demands of your specific application — structural, flexible, high-temperature, or cosmetic.

Ask About Materials
PLA / PLA+
General Purpose
Stiff, easy to print, wide colour range. Best for models and non-structural parts.
PETG
Tough & Chemical Resistant
Good impact resistance with food-safe options. Great all-rounder for functional parts.
ABS / ASA
UV & Heat Resistant
ASA handles outdoor and automotive environments. ABS is smoothable with acetone.
TPU / TPE
Flexible & Rubber-Like
Shore hardness from 85A to 95A. Used for gaskets, grips, shock absorbers.
Engineering Filaments
High Performance / Specialty
Nylon, Polycarbonate, PPS and PPA for demanding applications. Superior strength, heat resistance, and dimensional stability.
Resin (SLA)
High Detail Finish
Layer resolution to 25 microns. Available in standard, flexible, and castable formulations.

What sets us apart

Local & Responsive

Perth-based means real conversations, not ticket queues. We respond within hours and can meet in person for complex briefs.

File-to-Part Expertise

We review every file before printing. If there's a wall thickness issue or a gap that'll fail, we'll flag it — not just run it and charge you for a bad part.

End-to-End Capability

From raw file to finished, painted, assembled part — we handle the full production chain so you don't need to coordinate multiple suppliers.

Every part is inspected before it leaves the production floor. Dimensional checks, surface review, and layer adhesion inspection are standard, not optional.

Transparent Pricing
